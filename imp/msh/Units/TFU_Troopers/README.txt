================================
TFU Stormtrooper pack by GGCTUK
================================

This is the TFU Stormtrooper pack I ported recently.

These come with a few variants and these are all override_texture compatible.

-Standard Stormtrooper (Included the "Endor mouth grille" texture version I used to create the Shock Trooper)
-Shocktrooper with backpack (ALA Survival missions)
-Shadowtrooper (Alternative texture included for old-school TFU version)
-Sand Trooper
-Magma Trooper (props to Anakin for allowing me the use of his backpack mesh)
